censor
======

Input: a string on its own line followed by a list of words on one or more
additional lines.

Output: the string, with each of the words found in the list replaced with as
many asterisks as it is long.

Sample input:
see, when you type hunter2, it doesn't show up as hunter2
you
hunter2

Sample output:
see, when *** type *******, it doesn't show up as *******
