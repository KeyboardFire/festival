wordcount
=========

Input: a series of one or more lines consisting of only the ASCII characters
a-z, all at least one character long.

Output: on separate lines, each unique word exactly once followed by a space
and the number of times the word occurred in the input.

Sample input:
foo
bar
bar
foo
foo
baz

Sample output:
foo 3
bar 2
baz 1

The output does not have to be in any particular order.
