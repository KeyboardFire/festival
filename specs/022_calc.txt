calc
====

Input: a mathematical expression consisting of numbers (with digits 0-9), the
operators +-*/, or parentheses.

Output: the result of evaluating the input.

Sample input:
12+3*4*(5-6)

Sample output:
0

/ is integer division, truncating the result (5/2 should result in 2). The
input will always form a valid expression and will never involve a division by
0.
