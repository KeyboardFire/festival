catbuzz
=======

Input: one or more positive integers, each on their own line.

Output: fizzbuzz run on the integers 1 through the input for each input line.

Sample input:
4
1
15

Sample output:
1
2
Fizz
4
1
1
2
Fizz
4
Buzz
Fizz
7
8
Fizz
Buzz
11
Fizz
13
14
Fizzbuzz

Ideally (if possible in the given language), in the spirit of the cat program,
the input should be read as a stream and the program should output immediately
after reading each line rather than printing all output when the program
terminates.
