textrot
=======

Input: a single line of text, consisting of only lowercase and uppercase
ASCII letters (a-zA-Z).

Output: the same line, followed by the line rotated left once, followed by the
line rotated twice, and so on, stopping immediately before the original line is
reached.

Sample input:
foobarbaz

Sample output:
foobarbaz
oobarbazf
obarbazfo
barbazfoo
arbazfoob
rbazfooba
bazfoobar
azfoobarb
zfoobarba
