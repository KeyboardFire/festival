differences
===========

Input: a list of integers, one per line.

Output: the integers separated by spaces, followed by the difference of each
two consecutive integers separated by spaces, followed by their second
differences, and so on until only one integer remains.

Sample input:
1
-4
3
-2
3
7
0

Sample output:
1 -4 3 -2 3 7 0
5 -7 5 -5 -4 7
12 -12 10 -1 -11
24 -22 11 10
46 -33 1
79 -34
113
