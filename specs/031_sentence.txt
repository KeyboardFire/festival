sentence
========

Input: a single line with one or more space-separated words, defined as one or
more of any character in a-z or A-Z, followed optionally by a period.

Output: the sentence with the first letter and letters directly after a period
capitalized and all other letters lowercase.

Sample input:
this is a sentence. HERE IS ANOTHER ONE. aNd AnOtHeR.

Sample output:
This is a sentence. Here is another one. And another.

The last word will always be followed by a period.
