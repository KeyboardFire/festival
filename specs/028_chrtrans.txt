chrtrans
========

Input: two lines, the first with an integer and the second with a single note.

Output: the note, chromatically transposed by as many half steps as the input
number.

Sample input:
-4
F#

Sample output:
D

All notes not in the C scale will be given and should be output with sharps,
not flats. The list of valid notes, in order, is:

 C
 C#
 D
 D#
 E
 F
 F#
 G
 G#
 A
 A#
 B
